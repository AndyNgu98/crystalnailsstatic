Policy

REFUNDS

All purchases and services are final you will NOT be refunded, if you are not content, speak with your technician and we will try our best to solve the issue.

ISSUE

If you are unsatisfied with your nails we will correct it for you, provided that you inform us immediately.

RIGHTS

We have the right to refuse serivce, and will not tolerate any disrespect towards any member of staff. If you have any sort of nail condition we have the rights to refuse treatment.

PRICING

We reserve the right to alter prices without prior notice.

HEALTH CONDITIONS

If you have any issues which could affect your treatment please let us know upon arrival.

Covid-19

Please arrive 10-15 minutes prior to your time slot and wait outside until called in and if you need to cancel your appointment please inform us at least 24 hours before If you are running late you must contact us asap.

If not your appointment will be automatically cancelled. All clients will be required to wash their hands prior and after the service as well as wear a face mask throughout. Strictly no extra guests as their will be waiting area (Customer use only).
